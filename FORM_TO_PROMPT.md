# Form-to-Prompt Converter

Use this guide to turn a completed CLIENT_INTAKE_FORM into the AI prompt.

---

## HOW TO USE THIS

1. Client fills out CLIENT_INTAKE_FORM.md
2. You copy the template below
3. Fill in each [FORM #] with the answer from that question number
4. Paste the completed prompt into Claude
5. Claude generates your copy

---

## PROMPT TEMPLATE

```
Generate complete web copy for my StoryBrand conversion template. Use the information below to create persuasive, mobile-first copy that drives the single conversion action: photo upload for quote.

## CLIENT INFORMATION

**Company Name:** [FORM #1]

**Industry/Service Type:** [FORM #2]

**Phone Number:** [FORM #3]

**Target Customer:** [FORM #5]

**Geographic Service Area:** [FORM #4]

## THE PROBLEM (What pain does the customer have?)

**Main Problem:** [FORM #6]

**Why Other Solutions Fail:** [FORM #7]

**Consequences of Not Solving:** [FORM #8]

## YOUR SOLUTION (What makes you different?)

**Core Differentiator:** [FORM #9]

**How You Do It:** [FORM #10]

**Speed/Timeline:** [FORM #11]

**Pricing Approach:** [FORM #12]

## SERVICES (Pick 3 main service categories)

**Service 1:** [FORM #13 Service 1 Name] - [FORM #13 Service 1 Description]

**Service 2:** [FORM #13 Service 2 Name] - [FORM #13 Service 2 Description]

**Service 3:** [FORM #13 Service 3 Name] - [FORM #13 Service 3 Description]

## TONE/VOICE

**Personality:** [FORM #14 - describe based on checkboxes]

**Key Message:** [FORM #18]

---

## OUTPUT NEEDED

Generate the following copy blocks for the template:

1. **Hero Headline** (8-12 words, punchy, benefit-driven)
2. **Hero Subtext** (One sentence explaining the value prop)
3. **Problem Section Text** (2-3 sentences about the customer's pain)
4. **Solution Section Text** (2-3 sentences about how you solve it)
5. **Service 1, 2, 3 Titles and Descriptions** (Title + 1 sentence each)
6. **CTA Button Text** (2-3 words, action-oriented)
7. **Form Intro Text** (One sentence for the photo upload CTA section)

Keep it conversational, direct, and mobile-friendly. No fluff. Maximum persuasion, minimum words.
```

---

## EXAMPLE: FILLED CONVERSION

**Client fills out form with:**
- #1: "Clean Slate Property Services"
- #2: "Overgrown property clearing and lot cleanup"
- #3: "(270) 555-0123"
- etc...

**You create prompt:**
```
**Company Name:** Clean Slate Property Services
**Industry/Service Type:** Overgrown property clearing and lot cleanup
**Phone Number:** (270) 555-0123
```

Then paste completed prompt into Claude → Get copy → Apply to template → Done.

---

## TIME ESTIMATE

- Client fills form: 15-20 minutes
- You convert to prompt: 3-5 minutes
- Claude generates copy: 30 seconds
- You apply to template: 5 minutes
- Rebrand with logo: 2 minutes

**Total: ~25-30 minutes per client site**
